Our Story

We are local company based out of the Kitsap Peninsula and have over 15 years of experience in the fence industry. We strive for excellence in all we do. Specializing in all types of fencing, such as: Chain link, both galvanized and vinyl, All types of Cedar (including custom design), Vinyl, Aluminum, Ornamental Iron, Agricultural, Split Rail. We also, build decks to specification. No job is too big or too small. Both residential and commercial.
